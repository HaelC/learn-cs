### Overview

<blockquote cite="https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.824/">
  6.824 is a core 12-unit graduate subject with lectures, readings, programming
  labs, an optional project, a mid-term exam, and a final exam. It will present
  abstractions and implementation techniques for engineering distributed
  systems. Major topics include fault tolerance, replication, and consistency.
  Much of the class consists of studying and discussing case studies of
  distributed systems.
</blockquote>

**Prerequisites**: 6.004 and one of 6.033 or 6.828, or equaivalent.
